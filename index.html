About Us

UpStage is a quarterly series of events and one on one interviews, hosted by Stage Investment. The intent of these events is to showcase Los Angeles' brightest and most exciting entrepreneurs in the digital media and technology space, as well as foster leadership and networking opportunities amongst entrepreneurs, VCs and influencers.

STAGE INVESTMENT IS A LOS ANGELES BASED, EARLY STAGE VENTURE CAPITAL FUND, FOCUSED ON DIGITAL MEDIA AND ENTERTAINMENT TECHNOLOGY COMPANIES.

Giving Back

TICKET PROCEEDS NOT ONLY ALLOW FOR THESE EVENTS TO BE FUN AND INTERESTING FOR ATTENDEES, BUT LEND AN ADDED BENEFIT OF GENERATING FUNDS FOR CHARITABLE DONATION. STAGE HAS DEEP CONNECTIONS TO THE AT RISK YOUTH COMMUNITIES IN LOS ANGELES, AND THEREFORE HAS CHOSEN TO DONATE A PORTION OF THESE FUNDS TO:

  • South Central Scholars
  • International Trade Education Programs
  • Los Angeles Leadership Academy

THROUGH OUR PARTNERSHIPS WITH THESE ORGANIZATIONS, WE HOPE TO M AKE A SUBSTANTIAL IMPACT AND AFFECT CHANGE ON THE LIVES OF FUTURE GENERATIONS OF CHILDREN
