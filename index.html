About us

UP STAGE is a bi-monthly program of one on one intimate interviews with some of the most successful and disruptive entrepreneurs in the digital media and technology space.

Created by the leading Los Angeles-based digital media and technology venture capital firm, Stage Investment, and supported by The British American Business Council Los Angeles, The UP STAGE brings truly insightful and innovative content to its audience.

Our audience and membership are leading and influential members of the Los Angeles business community. From entertainment to law, our attendees are the decision makers at their respective organizations.

Giving Back

STAGE UP events benefit at risk youth in our community. Through our partnerships with three impactful organizations we have made substantial impact in the lives of children of our future.

  • South Central Scholars
  • International Trade Education Programs
  • Los Angeles Leadership Academy

All profits from our events are donated to the above programs.
